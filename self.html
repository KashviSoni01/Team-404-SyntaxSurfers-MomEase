Nutrition Plan for Postpartum Moms

Key Nutrients for Recovery:

  • Protein: Helps in healing and muscle recovery. Includes foods like chicken, eggs, and beans.
  • Iron: Important for energy and preventing anemia. Found in spinach, lean meats, and lentils.
  • Calcium: Supports bone health, particularly important during breastfeeding. Found in dairy, tofu, and leafy greens.
  • Omega-3 Fatty Acids: Great for brain health and reducing inflammation. Found in salmon, flaxseeds, and walnuts.

Healthy Meal Suggestions:

  • Grilled chicken with quinoa and steamed broccoli
  • Salmon with sweet potatoes and a green salad
  • Lentil stew with whole-grain bread
  • Greek yogurt with berries and chia seeds
